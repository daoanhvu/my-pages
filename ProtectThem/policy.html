Private Policy For ProtectThem

This Privacy Policy is meant to help you understand what information we collect, why we collect it, and how you can update, manage, export, and delete your information.

Introduction

ProtectThem is an application to protect your secrets. Instead of keeping all of them in your memory and forget some of them regularly or keep them in a text file that is completely insecured, ProtectThem will secure them behide an strong layer of encryption.

What kind of information you will input to the application

All sensitive or private information that you must keep with you such as username and password for pages, phone numbers, bank accounts...

Information collection

We do not collect any single piece of your information. Information you input to the application will be encrypted with a strong algorithm and stored locally. The application provides you an option to backup your encrypted data to your Google Drive, this function will encrypt your information one more time and post to your Google Drive.

GPS Location

This application does not work with GPS location.
